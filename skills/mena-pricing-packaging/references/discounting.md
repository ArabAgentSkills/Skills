# Discounting

## Safer Wording

- "Save [amount] with annual billing" only when the math is exact.
- "Introductory price valid until [date]" when the date and renewal terms are clear.
- "Discount applies according to [policy]" when tax treatment has been reviewed.
- "Subject to eligibility and contract approval" for enterprise, reseller, or partner discounts.

## High-Risk Wording

- "VAT-free".
- "We pay your VAT".
- "Guaranteed more sales".
- "Everyone is approved".
- "No fees" without source-backed provider/customer-fee terms.
- Fabricated scarcity or deceptive countdowns.

## Review Gates

- Tax/legal review for VAT, coupons, cashback, referral rewards, installment discounts, regulated products, and consumer-finance wording.
- Native review for Arabic offer copy.
- Explicit approval before publishing or activating discounts.

## Evidence

- Promotions should be treated as hypotheses unless supported by merchant analytics, official provider source, or current market/category evidence.
