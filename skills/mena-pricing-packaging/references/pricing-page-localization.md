# Pricing Page Localization

## Required Inputs

- Country, buyer type, pricing surface, currency, billing period, tax context, and sales motion.
- Whether the page is self-serve, assisted, demo-first, quote-first, reseller, or procurement-led.

## Review Checks

- Price, currency, billing period, tax treatment, setup fee, implementation fee, add-ons, usage fees, support, and cancellation terms are clear.
- Public price points have source URL and checked date if referencing competitors or existing vendors.
- Annual savings, free trial, no-credit-card, and usage limits are true and supportable.
- Arabic copy has native review for public use.

## Confidence Labels

- High: official or current first-party source for a narrow fact.
- Medium: repeated public page pattern, still not a universal buyer preference.
- Low: market hypothesis requiring interviews or live analytics.
- Unknown: missing or stale public evidence.

## Avoid

- Exact competitor prices without current source checks.
- Claims about all Arab buyers, GCC buyers, or MENA SMEs.
- Legal/tax certainty from page examples alone.
