# Pricing Packaging Integration Checklist

## Answer Shape

- Files read.
- Source-backed facts.
- Pricing hypotheses.
- Unknowns.
- Review gates.
- Validation steps.
- Production launch gate.

## Review Steps

- Confirm country, buyer type, sales motion, pricing surface, currency, billing period, and tax context.
- Check exact prices, discounts, fees, VAT wording, BNPL/installment terms, and procurement fields against sources.
- Route payment, BNPL, tax, open banking, payroll, and identity implementation to owning skills.

## Production launch gate

- Use sandbox/test workflows before production checkout or pricing automation.
- Protect secrets, credentials, customer data, quote documents, and pricing models.
- Require explicit approval before publishing pricing, sending quotes, launching offers, activating discounts, or submitting tenders.
