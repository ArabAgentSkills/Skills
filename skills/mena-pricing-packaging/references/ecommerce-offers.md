# Ecommerce Offers

## Price Display

- Show original price, discounted price, savings, tax treatment, shipping, COD/payment fees, installment fees, and total payable before checkout.
- Avoid surprise fees at payment step.
- Confirm whether consumer-facing displayed prices need VAT-inclusive presentation.

## BNPL And Installments

- Use provider-specific, contract-specific language.
- Safe framing: eligible customers may use provider installments subject to provider approval, merchant agreement, and local terms.
- Confirm merchant fees, customer fees, settlement timing, refunds, returns, and disclosures in provider terms.

## Promotions

- Keep terms, expiry, eligibility, and tax treatment clear.
- Avoid fake urgency and hidden conditions.
- Do not claim "no fees" or "no risk" unless a source supports the exact market and provider.

## Routing

- BNPL implementation: `mena-bnpl`.
- Payment behavior: `mena-payments`.
- Tax treatment: `arab-einvoicing-tax`.
