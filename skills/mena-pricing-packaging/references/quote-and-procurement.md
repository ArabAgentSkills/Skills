# Quote And Procurement

## Quote Fields

- Legal entity and seller contact.
- Buyer legal name and procurement contact.
- Quote number and quote valid until date.
- Country, currency, billing period, and tax treatment.
- Scope, modules, seats/users, branches, usage assumptions, and add-ons.
- Implementation, migration, training, support, SLA, and security notes.
- Payment terms, PO/invoice handling, renewal, cancellation, and acceptance process.

## Procurement Context

- Government and semi-government sales may require supplier registration, tender workflows, purchase orders, legal review, tax documents, and authorized signatories.
- Do not submit tenders, register suppliers, or send quotes without explicit approval.

## Confidence

- High confidence: official procurement portal mechanics for a narrow country/source.
- Medium confidence: common enterprise quote structure.
- Unknown: buyer-specific requirements unless tender documents or buyer instructions are provided.

## Safety

- Keep quotes and procurement documents private unless approved.
- Do not invent legal entity, tax registration, bank, or authority-to-sign details.
