# SaaS Pricing

## Common Regional Page Patterns

- Monthly/annual toggles.
- Annual savings or annual-prepaid options.
- Free trial or freemium for low-touch products.
- Local currencies for local SME products.
- Enterprise or custom tier for larger buyers.
- Add-ons for users, branches, invoices, POS, channels, support, API, integrations, AI credits, or usage.

## Demo-First Triggers

- Payroll, finance, payments, BNPL, identity, tax, open banking, health, government procurement, or regulated data.
- Complex onboarding, data migration, hardware, custom integrations, SLA, or security review.
- Usage or customer size materially changes cost.

## Public Price Rules

- Source-backed public prices are snapshots, not timeless facts.
- Store checked date and source URL.
- Do not calculate final prices from macro indicators.

## Free Trial Rules

- Safer when sample data or guided setup proves value.
- Risky when live customer, payroll, tax, identity, bank, or payment data is needed.
