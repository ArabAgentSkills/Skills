# Ramadan

## Date Basis

- Treat future Ramadan start and end dates as `estimated` until an official country source confirms them.
- Store estimated windows separately from confirmed dates.
- Record country, source scope, confidence, last checked date, and refresh-by date.

## Planning Implications

- Ramadan can affect content tone, support hours, retail behavior, charity/CSR, food/family programming, logistics, banking, and customer service.
- Do not claim best posting times, conversion uplift, or category behavior without current country/category evidence.
- Egypt NTRA evidence is Egypt-specific planning context, not a region-wide rule.

## Review Gates

- Native/Muslim review for public copy.
- Legal/platform review for paid, outbound, regulated, financial, health, minors, or charity campaigns.
- Explicit approval before launch, send, publish, or audience upload.

## Avoid

- Trivializing fasting, prayer, worship, hardship, charity, or family themes.
- Turning religious observance into generic urgency.
- Unsupported claims about all Arab or Muslim users.
