# National Days

## Rules

- Store national days by country, official name, date basis, source URL, and observed holiday source.
- Fixed national dates can recur, but observed closures still need yearly refresh.
- Do not flatten national histories into pan-Arab copy.

## Sensitivity

- Flags, rulers, founders, military, security forces, independence, founding narratives, Palestine references, conflict references, and political slogans require country-native and legal/brand review.

## Campaign Guidance

- Use respectful, country-specific language.
- Avoid unsupported historical claims.
- Avoid using official marks, government logos, or protected symbols unless authorized.
- Require explicit approval before public, paid, or outbound launch.
