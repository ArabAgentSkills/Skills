# Eid Al-Adha

## Date Basis

- Eid al-Adha depends on Dhu al-Hijjah confirmation and must be country-scoped.
- Mark future dates as `estimated` until official confirmation.
- Saudi confirmation is central for Hajj timing, but holiday windows still vary by country and sector.

## Safety

- Treat sacrifice, Qurbani, pilgrimage, charity, meat, livestock, travel, and religious imagery as sensitive.
- Do not make religious rulings.
- Do not generalize official-sector dates to all businesses.

## Review Gates

- Native/Muslim review for public copy.
- Legal/compliance review for regulated services, donations, travel, finance, health, food, or livestock.
- Explicit approval before launch, send, publish, or production schedule changes.
