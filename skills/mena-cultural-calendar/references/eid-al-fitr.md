# Eid Al-Fitr

## Date Basis

- Eid al-Fitr is lunar and country-confirmed.
- Mark future dates as `estimated` or `library_estimated` until an official local announcement confirms the date and holiday window.
- Keep holiday start/end, source authority, and sector scope separate.

## Scope Risks

- Government, private sector, banks, schools, exchanges, malls, and logistics providers can have different closure windows.
- An official government holiday notice does not automatically prove bank, private-sector, or retail operations.

## Campaign Notes

- Gifting, apparel, food, family, charity, travel, and return-to-normal-hours themes are hypotheses unless source-backed for the market and category.
- Public copy needs native review.
- Paid/outbound activity needs platform and legal review plus explicit approval.
