# Calendar Refresh Rules

## Refresh Cadence

- Lunar religious dates: refresh during early planning, then again close to the event, then update after official confirmation.
- Official annual announcements: refresh for the stated year and source scope.
- Fixed national days: refresh observed closures yearly.
- School calendars: refresh each academic year and by school sector.
- Retailer-led shopping seasons: refresh from current retailer/platform/festival sources before campaign planning.
- Salary/payroll notes: refresh policy sources and avoid individual-level targeting assumptions.

## Certainty Labels

- `fixed`: recurring Gregorian date, still refresh observed closures.
- `official_announced`: official source announced a date/window for a stated year/scope.
- `official_confirmed`: official local source confirmed a lunar date/window.
- `estimated`: planning estimate, not final.
- `library_estimated`: generated by a library or generic API, not official confirmation.
- `sector_only`: bank, exchange, school, ministry, or similar sector source.
- `unknown`: no reliable current public source.

## Required Output

Every plan must show date basis, source scope, confidence, last checked date, and refresh-by date.
