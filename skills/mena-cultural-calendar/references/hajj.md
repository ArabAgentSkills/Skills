# Hajj

## Source Rule

- Use Saudi official Hajj sources and Dhu al-Hijjah confirmation for timing.
- Keep Hajj operational planning separate from Eid al-Adha holiday windows.

## Operational Impacts

- Hajj can affect travel, visas, logistics, hotels, payments, medical services, telecom, remittances, support hours, and customer movement in Saudi Arabia, especially Makkah, Madinah, and Jeddah.

## Safety

- Do not turn Hajj into generic sales urgency.
- Do not make religious rulings or claim eligibility, permit, visa, or ritual requirements without official sources.
- Public Hajj messaging requires Muslim/native review and legal/compliance review for regulated sectors.

## Output Fields

- country: Saudi Arabia or impacted market.
- source_scope: official Hajj source, logistics source, support policy, or campaign planning.
- date_basis: estimated or official confirmed.
- review_required: native, religious, legal, operations, or platform.
