# Levantine Arabic

## Use Cases

Use `Levantine Arabic` only as a draft-stage family label for early copy exploration across Jordan, Palestine, Lebanon, or Syria. Split by market before publication.

## Required Caution

- Treat all Levantine copy as `draft/native_review_required`.
- Do not assume one Levantine voice fits all markets, cities, communities, or channels.
- Avoid political, national, conflict, religious, and cultural references unless locally reviewed.
- Use MSA for formal, sensitive, regulated, or cross-market copy.

## Drafting Rules

- Keep drafts simple and low-idiom.
- Avoid slang-heavy CTAs unless the user provides approved examples.
- Ask whether the target is Jordan, Palestine, Lebanon, or Syria before finalizing.
- Keep support copy calm and concrete.

## Native Review Questions

- Which country and city/audience is this for?
- Does the phrasing sound too broad, too informal, or regionally mismatched?
- Are any words politically, socially, or class-coded?
- Should the final copy use MSA instead?
