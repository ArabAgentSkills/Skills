# Egyptian Arabic

## Use Cases

Egyptian Arabic can be useful for Egypt-first consumer, social, entertainment, education, local commerce, WhatsApp, support, and sales follow-up drafts when the brand wants an approachable tone.

## Required Caution

- Treat every Egyptian Arabic output as `draft/native_review_required`.
- Review slang intensity, humor, class signals, gendered language, and brand fit.
- Do not use Egyptian copy for other Arab markets without local review.
- Use MSA for formal, regulated, B2B, app UI, transactional, or compliance-adjacent copy unless the product strategy says otherwise.

## Drafting Rules

- Keep the wording simple before using slang.
- Avoid jokes, idioms, sarcasm, and cultural references unless supplied or approved by a native reviewer.
- Keep product names, legal names, plan names, URLs, and codes unchanged.
- Do not imitate a social class, age group, or influencer style.

## Native Review Questions

- Does this sound natural for the target city, age group, and channel?
- Is the level of informality acceptable for the brand?
- Are there class-coded, gendered, sarcastic, or pushy signals?
- Should any product terms stay in English?
