## Scope

Country: Qatar.
Segment: B2B SaaS, HR.
Channels: LinkedIn ads, search, localized landing page.
Languages: English and Arabic assumptions must be stated separately.
Timeframe: 30-day test planning window.

## Source-backed channel notes

| Country | Segment | Channel | Confidence | Evidence | Implication |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Qatar | B2B SaaS | LinkedIn ads | needs expert review | LinkedIn docs support location/profile-language mechanics; DataReportal gives country digital baseline. | Test only after local B2B/media-buyer review; document English versus Arabic targeting rationale. |
| Qatar | B2B SaaS | Search | unknown | Public sources do not prove HR SaaS search demand. | Run keyword and SERP research before budget. |
| Qatar | B2B SaaS | Landing page | needs native review | Public sources do not prove Arabic/English decision-maker preference. | Localize trust fields and validate language with local seller/native review. |

## Recommended first tests

1. Search intent test: English and Arabic keyword research, SERP review, and landing-page message test.
2. LinkedIn test: one narrow role/industry/location test with documented profile-language assumptions.
3. Partnership discovery: shortlist HR/payroll/accounting ecosystem partners; no commercial commitment.

## Unknowns

- Qatar HR SaaS search volume and keyword intent.
- Decision-maker language preference by company type.
- Procurement norms and partner access.
- Local legal/privacy requirements for lead forms and CRM upload.

## Validation steps

- QA English/Arabic landing page, lead form, privacy, and analytics.
- Confirm platform policy and lead-form consent wording.
- Set budget cap, stop rule, and weekly review.
- Capture explicit approval before launch, audience upload, spend, or partnership outreach.
