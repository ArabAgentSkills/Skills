# Partnerships

Use this reference for channel partnerships, reseller tests, marketplace partners, creators as partners, associations, events, and B2B ecosystem routes.

## Partnership Scope

Every partnership recommendation must state:

- Country.
- Segment.
- Partner type.
- Channel.
- Evidence.
- Confidence.
- Review status.
- Commercial commitment and approval gate.

## Scoped Notes

- Country: Qatar/UAE/Kuwait. Segment: B2B/enterprise/expat services. Channel: partnerships and LinkedIn/search. Confidence: needs expert review. Evidence: country digital baselines and LinkedIn mechanics do not establish procurement norms. Agent implication: get local B2B seller or partner operator review before prioritizing.
- Country: Morocco. Segment: SaaS/ecommerce/public sector. Channel: partnerships/search/content. Confidence: needs expert review. Evidence: digital strategy context only. Agent implication: validate language, sector access, and partner credibility locally.
- Country: Multi-market Arab/MENA. Segment: regulated categories. Channel: partnerships. Confidence: needs expert review. Evidence: regulatory and platform risks vary. Agent implication: legal/compliance review before partner claims, co-marketing, referrals, or data sharing.

## Partnership Test Shape

1. Partner hypothesis.
2. Country and segment fit.
3. Mutual offer and customer path.
4. Data-sharing and privacy review.
5. Co-marketing claims and brand approval.
6. Small test metric.
7. No commercial commitment until explicit approval.
