# WhatsApp Commerce

Use this reference for WhatsApp as a commerce, support, lead nurture, or CRM channel.

## Scope And Risk

Country/segment/channel confidence: A for WhatsApp platform mechanics from official docs; needs expert review for live direct marketing, consent, pricing, and local privacy execution.

## Required Gates

- Explicit opt-in and consent proof.
- Approved template category where a template is required.
- Country pricing and billing review.
- Quality, rate-limit, and business account status check.
- Stop/unsubscribe handling.
- Data minimization and privacy notice.
- Sandbox/test send where available.
- Explicit approval before production send.

## Avoid

- Scraping contacts.
- Sending without opt-in.
- Claiming template approval or country pricing without live check.
- Reusing support conversations for marketing without consent review.
- Storing message bodies or personal data unnecessarily.
