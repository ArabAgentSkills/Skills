# Marketplaces

Use this reference for marketplace channel tests, marketplace-led promotions, app store surfaces, and ecommerce distribution.

## Scope

Marketplace channel notes must be country, category, channel, confidence, evidence, and source scoped. If source evidence is unavailable, mark `needs expert review`.

## Marketplace Test Checklist

- Country/category fit source or expert review.
- Listing language and image QA.
- Local payment, delivery, return, refund, and support terms.
- Marketplace policy and claims review.
- Inventory and fulfillment readiness.
- Budget/promo approval before launch.

## Avoid

- Assuming White Friday, Ramadan, or national-day promotions apply equally across markets.
- Treating marketplace rankings, reviews, or badges as usable proof unless source and permission are clear.
- Publishing customer reviews or screenshots without consent.
