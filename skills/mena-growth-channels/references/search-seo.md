## Search Test Checklist

- Country and language-specific keyword set.
- SERP review by country and language.
- Localized landing page or content URL.
- Hreflang and language switcher where variants exist.
- Conversion event and analytics QA.
- Paid search policy review for sensitive categories and remarketing.
