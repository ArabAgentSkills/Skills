# MENA Partnerships and Distribution

Draft and review Arab/MENA partnership and distribution motions: reseller strategy, agency partnerships, systems integrators, telco/bank/payment/logistics ecosystems, partner pitches, and co-marketing.

## Scope

This skill supports planning, drafting, and QA. It does not approve partner terms, sign agreements, submit applications, grant access, publish announcements, or promise introductions.

## Source Philosophy

- Use official procurement portals, country commercial guides, regulator pages, platform policies, and public partner pages as constraints and examples.
- Treat partner-program pages as proof that a motion exists, not proof of terms, acceptance, pricing, exclusivity, licensing, or local feasibility.
- Mark commercial agency law, exclusivity, termination, commission, regulated data, payments, banking, telco, logistics, and government access as review-required.

## Maintenance

- Keep `SKILL.md` short and procedural.
- Update `sources.yml` when source URLs or confidence change.
- Put detailed partner workflows in `references/`, examples in `examples/`, and eval coverage in `evals/`.
- Run `pnpm skills:validate`, `pnpm validate`, `pnpm test`, and `pnpm site:build` before release readiness when shared repo state allows it.
