# Agency Partnerships

Use for marketing agencies, ecommerce agencies, CRM consultants, software houses, outsourced operations teams, and implementation agencies.

## Good-Fit Motions

- Referral partnership: agency introduces qualified opportunities; vendor owns sales and delivery.
- Implementation partnership: agency configures, localizes, trains, or integrates.
- Agency bundle: product is packaged into a service retainer.
- Co-marketing: webinar, guide, event, case study, or community distribution.

## Agency Pitch Ingredients

- Shared audience.
- Specific client problem.
- How the agency makes money.
- What the vendor handles.
- What the agency handles.
- Proof assets or pilot result.
- Onboarding path.
- Data and brand-use boundaries.

## Guardrails

- Avoid implying the agency can speak for every local market or buyer segment.
- Do not use the agency's customer names, logos, screenshots, dashboards, or metrics without permission.
- Do not transfer customer data, ad accounts, CRM records, WhatsApp chats, or production access until data-processing and security boundaries are reviewed.
- Co-branded public copy needs brand approval and native/operator review if Arabic, dialect, cultural, religious, or national references appear.

## Pilot Structure

1. Define one ICP and country.
2. Pick one offer bundle.
3. Agree lead handoff and ownership.
4. Agree implementation and support responsibilities.
5. Agree data boundaries.
6. Review results after a fixed period before expanding.
