# Telco and Bank Partnerships

Use for telco, communications-platform, bank, open-banking, open-finance, and regulated ecosystem partnership planning.

## Risk Position

Treat these as high-risk by default. A pitch is not enough. Regulated feasibility, security, data, procurement, licensing, compliance, and executive approval matter before partner outreach or commitments.

## Telco Partnership Checks

- Country and telecom regulatory context.
- Whether the motion involves messaging, sender IDs, numbers, SIM/eSIM, identity, location, subscriber data, billing, or API access.
- Platform policy, consent, opt-out, DND, and customer-communications boundaries.
- Data minimization and security review.
- No claims of telco access or approval unless documented.

## Bank and Open-Finance Checks

- Country regulator, framework, licensing, sandbox, and production route.
- Whether the motion involves account information, payment initiation, KYC, credit, consent, customer data, or money movement.
- Security, privacy, DPA, data residency, retention, audit, incident response, and revocation handling.
- No claims of bank approval, regulator approval, or open-banking readiness without source-backed evidence and expert review.

## Pitch Limits

Allowed:

- Problem statement.
- Shared customer hypothesis.
- Draft integration or pilot scope.
- Review gates and open questions.

Not allowed:

- Guaranteed access.
- Claims of licensing or approval.
- Requests for production credentials, real customer data, or customer lists.
- Unreviewed public logo use.

## Operator Review

Government, semi-government, central bank, telecom regulator, public utility, bank, or critical infrastructure mentions require operator and expert review before sending, publishing, or submitting anything.
