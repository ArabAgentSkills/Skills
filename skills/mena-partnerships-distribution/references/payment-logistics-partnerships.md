# Payment and Logistics Partnerships

Use for payment gateway, PSP, fintech, ecommerce logistics, delivery, fulfillment, and last-mile ecosystem partnerships.

## Payment Partnership Checks

- Country and regulator context.
- Payment flow: collection, payout, refund, split payment, subscription, BNPL, credit, wallet, card, bank transfer, or open banking.
- Licensing, merchant-of-record, settlement, chargeback, KYC/KYB, AML, fraud, tax, and data-processing responsibilities.
- Sandbox vs production, webhook security, idempotency, reconciliation, and audit logging.
- No live money movement or customer-data access without explicit approval.

## Logistics Partnership Checks

- Country, city coverage, service levels, returns, COD, customs, cold chain, regulated goods, and customer communications.
- Data shared: names, phone numbers, addresses, order contents, tracking events, and delivery instructions.
- Consent, minimization, retention, and support escalation.
- Operational ownership for failed delivery, returns, refunds, complaints, and SLA reporting.

## Public Source Use

Public payment and logistics partner pages can show that partner or integration motions exist. They do not prove acceptance criteria, pricing, settlement terms, legal obligations, or service coverage for a specific partnership.

## Review Gates

Require legal/compliance/security review for:

- Money movement, refunds, payouts, credit, BNPL, open banking, tax, KYC/KYB, or AML.
- Customer phone numbers, addresses, order contents, or regulated delivery data.
- Production API access, webhooks, shared dashboards, or support impersonation.
- Government, semi-government, bank, telco, health, identity, tax, payroll, or education customers.
