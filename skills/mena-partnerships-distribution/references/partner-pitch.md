# Partner Pitch

Use this to draft a partner pitch that is specific, conservative, and review-ready.

## Pitch Fields

- `partner_type`: referral, reseller, implementation, agency bundle, technology integration, co-marketing, regulated ecosystem.
- `country_or_market`: scoped market, not generic MENA unless the plan is truly regional.
- `shared_customer`: who both parties serve.
- `customer_problem`: specific pain.
- `joint_offer`: how the combined offer helps.
- `commercial_model`: referral fee, resale margin, services revenue, revenue share, co-funded campaign, or unknown.
- `operating_model`: ownership of sales, onboarding, support, compliance, billing, and customer success.
- `proof`: case study, source-backed market context, integration demo, pilot result, or approved reference.
- `approval_gates`: legal, finance, data, security, brand, regulatory, and executive approval.

## Draft Template

```text
Subject: Pilot partnership idea for [shared customer segment]

Hi [Name],

I am [Sender] from [Company]. We are exploring a pilot partnership around [specific customer problem] for [country/segment].

The working hypothesis is:

- Shared customer: [customer]
- Customer problem: [problem]
- Joint offer: [offer]
- Partner value: [why partner benefits]
- Vendor role: [vendor handles]
- Partner role: [partner handles]
- Pilot scope: [small, reversible pilot]

Before discussing commercial terms, I would want to confirm fit, customer-data boundaries, brand-use rules, and any legal/compliance review needed on both sides.

Would a short discovery call be useful?

Regards,
[Sender]
```

## Required Caveats

- Use no-exclusivity and pilot-first language unless reviewed.
- Do not promise government, bank, telco, chamber, regulator, or customer access.
- Do not mention customer names, logos, or case studies without permission.
- Mark country-specific tone, Arabic wording, seniority, and etiquette as native/operator review items.
