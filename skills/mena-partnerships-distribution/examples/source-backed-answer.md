## Recommendation

Treat this as a regulated ecosystem partnership, not a normal reseller pitch. Prepare a discovery checklist and pilot hypothesis, then require legal, compliance, security, data, regulator, and executive review before outreach or application submission.

## Source-backed notes

- Central bank and official framework pages support caution for open-banking and open-finance partnerships.
- Public partner pages show that ecosystem motions exist, but they do not prove acceptance, licensing, pricing, terms, or readiness.

## Unknowns

- Licensing route: Needs regulator review.
- Production access path: Unknown from public docs.
- Customer-data processing role: Needs legal/security review.
- Public logo or partner-name use: Needs brand approval.

## Draft next step

Create a one-page partnership hypothesis with shared customer, customer problem, pilot scope, data boundaries, approval gates, and open questions.

## Validation steps

- Confirm country, partner type, regulated domain, and commercial model.
- Confirm whether the motion touches money movement, bank data, telecom messages, identity, customer data, or government buyers.
- Keep any technical integration in sandbox until legal/compliance/security approval is complete.
- Review the final pitch with the operator before sending or submitting.
