# SME Sales

Use for merchants, clinics, agencies, education centers, logistics operators, ecommerce teams, local service firms, and founder-led businesses that can buy without a full tender.

## Workflow

1. Identify country, sector, company size, buyer owner, current pain, and channel permission.
2. Draft a short value hypothesis tied to observable business context.
3. Keep one primary CTA: reply, book a short call, confirm the right owner, or review a one-page proposal.
4. Add one useful asset in follow-up: calculator, checklist, case study, integration map, demo, or buyer-specific note.
5. If WhatsApp is requested, load `whatsapp-follow-up.md` and require prior context or opt-in.

## Message Defaults

- Short and specific.
- Practical proof before broad claims.
- Clear sender identity.
- Easy opt-out where outbound applies.
- No unsupported local norms, pricing, discounts, financing, or ROI guarantees.

## Discovery Questions

- What problem are you trying to fix this month?
- How is the workflow handled today?
- Who owns the decision and budget?
- What would make a trial safe for your team?
- Are there data, customer-contact, payment, tax, health, or legal constraints?

## Operator Review Flags

- Contact source is unclear.
- The target is a consumer or sensitive individual rather than a business contact.
- The draft includes pricing, discount, credit, financing, tax, health, legal, employment, immigration, or government claims.
- The user asks for bulk WhatsApp, SMS, email, directory scraping, or chamber-member outreach.
