# MENA Sales Playbooks

Draft and review Arab/MENA B2B sales playbooks for SME outbound, enterprise sales, WhatsApp follow-up, meeting follow-up, objection handling, procurement caution, and reseller/channel sales.

## Scope

This skill is for planning, drafting, and QA. It does not send outreach, submit procurement documents, register suppliers, approve partner terms, or claim local sales norms without evidence.

## Source Philosophy

- Use official procurement portals, ITA country commercial guides, platform policies, regulator pages, chambers, and ecosystem reports as constraints.
- Treat public market statistics as planning signals, not proof of buyer personality or local etiquette.
- Mark unsupported local tone, dialect, greeting, seniority, meeting cadence, and objection wording as `Needs native/operator review`.

## Maintenance

- Keep `SKILL.md` short and procedural.
- Update `sources.yml` when source URLs or confidence change.
- Put detailed playbooks in `references/`, examples in `examples/`, and eval coverage in `evals/`.
- Run `pnpm skills:validate`, `pnpm validate`, `pnpm test`, and `pnpm site:build` before release readiness when shared repo state allows it.
