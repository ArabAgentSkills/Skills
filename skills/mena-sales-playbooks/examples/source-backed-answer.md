## Recommendation

Treat this as an enterprise procurement-aware sales motion, not a generic outbound sequence. Draft a short capability note and discovery agenda, then verify supplier registration, tender route, security review, and legal/procurement owners before any formal proposal.

## Source-backed notes

- Official procurement portals and ITA country guides support using country-specific procurement caution.
- Public sources do not justify precise local etiquette, dialect tone, or universal buyer behavior claims.

## Unknowns

- Buyer procurement route: Unknown from public docs.
- Supplier registration requirement: Needs procurement review.
- Arabic wording and seniority etiquette: Needs native/operator review.
- WhatsApp follow-up permission: Unknown from public docs until the contact source and opt-in are confirmed.

## Draft next step

Prepare a discovery agenda covering current workflow, decision owners, procurement route, security/legal review, data boundaries, and budget timing.

## Validation steps

- Confirm country, buyer type, sector, and sales stage.
- Verify contact source and channel permission.
- Check whether the buyer is government, semi-government, regulated, or procurement-led.
- Review the final draft with the operator before sending.
