## Scope

Country: Saudi Arabia.
Segment: ecommerce, B2C.
Channels: paid social, search, WhatsApp, landing page.
Languages: Arabic and English drafts; final Arabic requires native review.
Timeframe: Ramadan/Eid planning; Hijri dates and sector closures require official refresh before launch.

## Localization decisions

- Landing page: visible SAR pricing, delivery/returns, payment options, privacy, support language, and Ramadan support-hour notes.
- Paid social: neutral value framing; no personal religious attribute, hardship, or fasting-based targeting.
- Search: separate Arabic and English keyword research and landing pages if demand supports it.
- WhatsApp: opt-in proof, approved template category, stop/unsubscribe wording, pricing/rate-limit check, and explicit approval before send.

## Unknowns

- Current Saudi Ramadan/Eid official dates and sector closure windows for the launch year.
- Category-specific conversion, creative timing, and channel performance.
- Native review outcome for Arabic phrasing and religious tone.
- Local legal/privacy review for CRM/direct marketing.

## Validation steps

- QA mobile Arabic/English landing pages.
- Check ad and landing policy together.
- Test UTMs, pixels, consent, and forms in sandbox/test mode.
- Review inventory, delivery, payment settlement, return policy, and support hours.
- Capture explicit approval before paid launch, audience upload, creator publication, or WhatsApp send.
