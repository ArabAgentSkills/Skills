# Search And SEO

Use this reference for Arabic, English, French, bilingual, and multi-country search work.

## SEO Mechanics

Country/segment/channel confidence: A for mechanics. Evidence: Google Search Central multilingual and multi-regional guidance.

Required:

- Separate URLs for language or country variants.
- Hreflang annotations where variants target different languages or regions.
- Obvious page language.
- Visible language switchers.
- Do not auto-redirect in a way that hides variants from crawlers or users.

## Keyword Research Rules

- Country: required.
- Segment: required.
- Channel: organic search or paid search.
- Language: Arabic, English, French, dialect, bilingual, or unknown.
- Confidence: keyword tools and SERP reviews are planning evidence, not cultural proof.
- Review: native SEO review required for dialect, slang, transliteration, Arabizi, and French/Arabic tradeoffs.

## Paid Search

- Split Arabic and English campaigns only when keyword, landing page, and ad copy are localized.
- Avoid sensitive personalized categories and restricted remarketing.
- Do not claim search volume proves buying intent unless the source and timeframe support it.
- Check landing-page relevance, currency, claims, and privacy before launch.
