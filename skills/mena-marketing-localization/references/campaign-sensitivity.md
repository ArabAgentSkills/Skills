# Campaign Sensitivity

Use this reference for high-risk campaign, copy, creator, and localization reviews.

## High-Risk Flags

- Religion.
- National symbolism.
- Politics or conflict.
- Ethnicity or nationality.
- Gender or family status.
- Minors or school context.
- Health.
- Financial hardship, credit, lending, salary, or debt.
- Employment or housing.
- Identity, government, tax, legal, or regulated products.
- Privacy, personal data, CRM uploads, or direct marketing.
- Creator, testimonial, or paid partnership.
- Dialect, humor, idioms, or native copy.

## Review Rules

- Country/segment/channel confidence: needs expert review for legal, privacy, regulated, religious, political, national-symbol, and platform-policy interpretation unless an official source and qualified review are recorded.
- Country/segment/channel confidence: needs native review for dialect, humor, idioms, slogans, public Arabic/French copy, and cultural references.
- Public statistics are planning signals, not stereotypes.
- Creator, outbound, and paid campaign actions require explicit approval before live execution.

## Safer Rewrites

| Risky pattern | Safer direction |
| --- | --- |
| Personal hardship | Neutral benefit framing. |
| Religious pressure | Respectful utility or service information. |
| National symbolism | Factual event mention plus native review. |
| Dialect joke | Plain MSA draft plus native review. |
| Urgent scarcity | Verifiable availability and clear terms. |
| Hidden sponsorship | Visible disclosure and platform label. |

## Automatic Fail Conditions

- Broad claims about Arab people, religions, dialect groups, nationalities, gender, age, income, or class.
- Invented country, cultural, legal, religious, platform, calendar, or performance facts.
- Final dialect or sensitive copy without review status.
- PII, raw survey responses, private screenshots, customer lists, or reviewer identities without consent.
- Instructions to launch, send, upload, or publish without explicit approval.
