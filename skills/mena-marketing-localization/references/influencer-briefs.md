# Influencer Briefs

Use this reference for creator, influencer, ambassador, testimonial, or paid partnership briefs.

## Required Brief Fields

| Field | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Country | Required; multi-country creator plans need country-by-country notes. |
| Segment | B2C, ecommerce, app, marketplace, SaaS, regulated, youth/minors, or other. |
| Channel | TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Snapchat, LinkedIn, or other. |
| Language | MSA, dialect, English, French, bilingual, or unknown. |
| Disclosure | Required for paid/value exchange content. |
| Claims | Substantiate product, price, savings, ranking, health, finance, employment, or testimonial claims. |
| Rights | Usage duration, territories, channels, whitelisting, edits, exclusivity, and takedown terms. |
| Review | Native, legal, platform, brand, and category review as needed. |

## Scoped Notes

- Country: Multi-market Arab/MENA. Segment: B2C/ecommerce/app/marketplace. Channel: TikTok/Instagram/YouTube creators. Confidence: A for disclosure mechanics. Evidence: TikTok branded content policy, Instagram branded content help, YouTube paid promotion help. Agent implication: include disclosure and platform review in every creator brief.
- Country: Morocco. Segment: marketplace launch. Channel: creator/influencer. Confidence: needs native review. Evidence: public digital context does not establish dialect, French/Arabic mix, or creator norm. Agent implication: require Moroccan native marketer and local legal/platform review.
- Country: Saudi Arabia. Segment: regulated, finance, health, charity, religious-season campaigns. Channel: creator. Confidence: needs expert review. Evidence: high-risk category and religious/national context require local expert/legal review. Agent implication: do not approve final script without scoped review.

## Creator Safety Checklist

- Disclosure wording and platform label.
- Claims substantiation and prohibited claims.
- No undisclosed incentives.
- No minors, health, finance, employment, housing, identity, or religious claims without expert review.
- No political or national-symbol messaging without local review.
- Usage rights and whitelisting documented.
- No publication until explicit approval.
