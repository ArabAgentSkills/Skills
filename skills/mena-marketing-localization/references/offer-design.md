# Offer Design

Use this reference for discounts, bundles, free trials, Ramadan/Eid offers, national-day promotions, marketplace incentives, and B2B trials.

## Offer Scope

Every offer note must include:

- Country.
- Segment.
- Channel.
- Timeframe.
- Offer mechanics.
- Evidence or hypothesis status.
- Confidence.
- Legal/platform/native review status.

## Offer Evidence Rules

- Public market reports can justify research priority, not conversion promises.
- Platform policies can define what ads can say, not what customers prefer.
- Retailer-led events are commercial hypotheses unless current country/category evidence supports them.
- Salary-period ideas are high risk and must not imply personal income, debt, hardship, or vulnerability.

## Scoped Notes

- Country: UAE. Segment: ecommerce. Channel: landing page/paid social/search. Confidence: B. Evidence: EZDubai/Euromonitor release supports ecommerce market context and mentions payment, logistics, and returns. Agent implication: prioritize transparent delivery, return, and payment terms over unsupported discount claims.
- Country: Saudi Arabia. Segment: ecommerce/SaaS. Channel: Ramadan/Eid/National Day offers. Confidence: A for official calendar source, unknown for performance. Evidence: SAMA/MOFA source leads. Agent implication: use official dates and ops checks; do not claim offer uplift without category data.
- Country: Multi-market Arab/MENA. Segment: finance/credit. Channel: paid social/search/landing page. Confidence: needs expert review. Evidence: platform sensitive targeting rules and local legal risk. Agent implication: avoid urgency around hardship, debt, salary, or family pressure.

## Approval Gate

- Discount math and availability verified.
- Claims and testimonials substantiated.
- Payment, refund, return, cancellation, tax/VAT, and delivery terms checked.
- Regulated-category and local law review complete.
- Paid launch or public publish explicitly approved.
