# Market Expert Review

Market expert review is required when a regional skill makes strategic, commercial, compliance-adjacent, procurement, pricing, partnership, or operational recommendations.

## Reviewer Types

- Market operator
- Sales operator
- Support operator
- Marketer
- Founder/operator
- Legal or compliance specialist

## Review Scope

Reviewers should check the exact country, segment, channel, business model, and risk level. A review for one market or segment does not validate another.

## Approval Labels

- `not_reviewed`
- `reviewed_with_notes`
- `approved`
- `rejected`
- `needs_revision`

Do not use `market_validated` unless there is real customer, operator, survey, or experiment evidence.
