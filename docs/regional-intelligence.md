# Regional Intelligence Skills

Regional Intelligence Skills help agents work better across Arab markets without pretending to be local experts.

Use them for concrete tasks:

- Country context before launch, sales, support, localization, or campaign work.
- Arabic copy and dialect-choice drafts.
- Marketing localization, growth experiments, sales playbooks, support macros, product localization, pricing, and partnership planning.
- Calendar-aware planning around Ramadan, Eid, national days, and public holidays.

Do not use them for stereotypes, legal advice, political persuasion targeting, religious certainty, or final public dialect copy without review.

## Source Of Truth

- `data/regional/countries.public.json`
- `data/regional/regional-sources.public.json`
- `data/regional/regional-claims.public.json`
- `data/regional/review-status.public.json`
- `skills/arab-market-context/references/countries/*.md`
- `skills/*/evals/*.yml`

## Required Status Labels

- `AI drafted`: generated or maintained by agents.
- `Source checked`: public source links were attached.
- `Native review needed`: dialect, tone, idiom, public brand copy, or support copy needs local language review.
- `Expert review needed`: legal, tax, regulated, procurement, pricing, or strategic market claims need a specialist.
- `Market validation needed`: the claim needs survey, experiment, customer, or operator evidence before strategic use.

No page should claim native review, expert review, or market validation unless the public data records it.
