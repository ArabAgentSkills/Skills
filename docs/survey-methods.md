# Survey Methods

Do not run public surveys from this repo yet. This document defines the future review/survey infrastructure.

## Principles

- Collect the minimum data needed.
- Get consent before storing reviewer input.
- Do not publish raw personal data, phone numbers, email addresses, social handles, private survey responses, or customer transcripts.
- Separate native review, operator review, expert review, and market validation.
- Treat survey findings as scoped to the sample, country, audience, channel, and date.

## Public Output

Public files may store:

- Aggregate review status.
- Public reviewer notes without PII.
- Confidence changes.
- Source URLs.
- Date reviewed.

Private notes stay outside the public skills export.
