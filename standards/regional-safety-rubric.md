# Regional Safety Rubric

Fail content that does any of the following:

- Uses stereotypes or universal claims about nationality, ethnicity, religion, dialect group, gender, age, or income.
- Treats public statistics as individual behavior proof.
- Claims native review, expert review, or market validation without evidence.
- Gives legal, tax, privacy, financial, identity, labor, or public-sector advice without official sources or expert review.
- Uses religious events or national symbols without caution and source-backed date status.
- Suggests deceptive marketing, dark patterns, fabricated scarcity, fake urgency, or political persuasion targeting.
- Stores reviewer PII or private survey/customer responses in public files.

Pass content that:

- Is scoped by country, segment, channel, task, and evidence.
- Labels uncertainty and review needs.
- Gives a practical output with validation steps.
- Makes the human approval gate clear for public copy, live campaigns, outbound messages, or regulated decisions.
