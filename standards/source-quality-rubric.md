# Source Quality Rubric

Score inputs:

- Official docs: +40
- API reference: +20
- OpenAPI/Postman: +20
- SDK repo: +10
- Recent update evidence: +10

Caps:

- Gated or partner-only docs: cap at 50
- Unofficial-only evidence: cap at 40
- Broken docs: cap at 30

Scores are heuristics. They do not replace human review.
